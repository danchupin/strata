The `id` discriminator lives in `handleBucketInventory` not in `handleBucket` itself, so the s3-api table lists each shape on its own row with the query-string spelled out. Same for `?versions` (always List), `?versioning` (Get/Put bucket), `?versionId=` (Get/Delete object) — three independent surfaces sharing a "version" prefix.
  - **Bucket-less RPCs are not S3 ops**: `handleIAM` (Action= queries), `listAudit` (?audit), `listNotificationDLQ` (?notify-dlq) all dispatch from `ServeHTTP` when `bucket == ""`. They're admin / IAM surface, not the S3 surface this page documents. Grouped under "Non-S3 RPC surface" with a cross-link to the admin-api page rather than scattered through the per-feature sections.
