## 2026-05-04 â€” US-005 â€” OTel trace ring buffer + endpoint
- New `internal/otel/ringbuf` package â€” `RingBuffer` implements `sdktrace.SpanProcessor` (no-op `OnStart`/`Shutdown`/`ForceFlush`; `OnEnd` records every span). - Wired into `otel.Init` via `STRATA_OTEL_RINGBUF` (default `on`).
