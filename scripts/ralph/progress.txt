- **Learnings for future iterations:**
  - **Hugo `{{< ref >}}` forward-link blocker**: when authoring a multi-page set in sequence, the index page can't pre-reference unauthored siblings — the build fails `REF_NOT_FOUND`. Two workarounds: (a) author the index last, after the leaves exist; (b) use bare slug references with no shortcode until the leaves land, then swap.   - **Default-verification call sites**: defaults live in three places — `internal/config/config.go::defaults()` (gateway core), worker constructor `Build` funcs (`durationFromEnv("STRATA_FOO", default)`), and helper-specific `*FromEnv` functions (`internal/data/rados/parallel.go::putConcurrencyFromEnv`, `internal/otel/otel.go::ratioFromEnv`, `internal/s3api/audit.go::ParseAuditRetention`). When the env-vars table looks stale, re-grep these three patterns; don't trust the README.
  - **Test/fixture noise in the grep**: `grep -rhoE 'STRATA_[A-Z_][A-Z0-9_]+'` picks up test-fixture vars (`*_TEST`, `*_UNSET`, `*_INT64_TEST`) and storage-class strings (`STRATA_COLD` from a lifecycle test XML). Filter these out before adding rows — keeping only the call sites that aren't inside `_test.go` (or grouping them under `Test-only`) prevents operators from chasing phantom knobs.
